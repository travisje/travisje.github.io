A Beginner's Testing Example...

Aug 8th, 2015 8:03 pm

Ok, so I have a confession to make. I have been avoiding spec testing like the plague. I mean, it’s just so much fun be in the world of invention, where each keystroke heralds in a new feature or possibility. But, as we are in the second week of a project mode, I’ve realized that this fantasy quickly breaks down and becomes a nightmare. We recently had a speaker come in to Flatiron and tell us that he would never work for a company that didn’t perform tests and now I get it.

I am currently working on a fun app with my team that allows individuals to challenge each other in any type of competition and allows users of the site to vote on who wins. We’ve been building out various features on it, and have refactored our code, added more features, and then realized that a good chunk of our existing application was broken after the fact. So, I thoght now would be a good time to try to learn a few basics of testing. More to come soon!
